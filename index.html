Reality Brief:
Automated News

To curb the inherent bias in most news sources, Reality Brief is an attempt at indiscriminate, automated reporting. By making use of the Wikimedia and Sigimera APIs, it collects lists of the types of events that often make it into the news, and reports every time one of those events occures.

Socrates is Mortal:
A sketchbook for logicians

Socrates is Mortal is a platform for logical argument development. Users can create Syllogisms, and the app will determine the validity of those Syllogisms. Once created, arguments can be shared with others to demonstrate the reasoning behind a belief. The backend is built on Ruby on Rails, and data is managed in SQLite
